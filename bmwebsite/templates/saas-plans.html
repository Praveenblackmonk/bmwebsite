FAQ

  • Can I try BlackMonk before subscribing to a plan?

    Absolutely! You can request for a demo to start using BlackMonk. You will be notified by email with the login details of our demo site, where you can explore the features of BlackMonk.

    Note: The login details of our demo site will be accessible only for 3 days from the date of login.

  • How do I buy the Hosted Edition license?

    You can choose a plan and select the billing type (monthly/yearly). After you choose a plan, you will be asked to enter your account info and redirected to the payment gateway.

  • How do I request for Enterprise Edition?

    BlackMonk CMS Enterprise Edition enables you to access full source code and you can request for any feature/design customization.Please click on the "Request a Quote" button and submit.

  • Do we get to choose from different themes?

    Yes! Each plan has set of predefined themes tailored to suit your business. Choose a template based on your goals, content, and taste. This is an important decision, so make sure you choose the right template.

  • Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

    There’s no contract on the monthly packages — you just pay as you go, and you can cancel any time without penalty. The Annual Package is a one-year contract billed month to month.

  • Do I need another web host?

    No. All BlackMonk plans include our fully-managed cloud hosting, ensuring your website remains available at all times.

  • How do I accept payments with BlackMonk Commerce?

    BlackMonk is tightly integrated with PayPal, Authorize.net and Stripe for accepting payments from your website users. The payments are directly credited to the payment gateway you configured in your website.

  • How do I cancel the service?

    You may cancel your subscription at any time by sending an email to support@blackmonk.com with your intent of cancellation.

  • I dont have a domain name. Will you be providing a subdomain for my website?

    No ! We do not provide a sub-domain.
